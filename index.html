About Us

Our Company

WeConstruct is an Engineering and Construction company situated in Wakanda, East Africa.

Our main focus is to render Building services varing from Foundation, to Building Construction to Plumbling design, Electrical Installations, Interior and Exterior finishes.

We have a team of professionals who are skilled and experienced in different engineerng related fields. We also offer Consultancy services for contractors and fabricate locally made materials.

Our strength lirs in our versatility and genuine interesrt in satisfying our clientsby utilizing locally sourced materials in a cost effective manner.

Our Core Value

When choosing our core values, we consider practices that will make our business sucessful and renowned. They include happiness/passion ,education, innovation/creativity, transparrency, excellent, giving back and safety.

Our Mission

To provide innovative approaches to the construction engineering industry in areas varying from materials selection, design and specification, electrical wiring and installation, plumbing, logistics, and to build effective relationship and deliver excellent services to our clinents in a timelly and cost effective manner.
